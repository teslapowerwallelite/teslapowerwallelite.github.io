The Command Center

Intuitive Elite Dashboard

Experience the most comprehensive view of your energy ecosystem. Our main interface provides a high-resolution, real-time power flow HUD. Monitor solar yield, battery health, and grid interaction with zero latency. Designed for clarity, the dashboard ensures you understand your home's energy status at a single glance.

Immediate Control

Forced Solar & Active Charge

Take absolute command of your energy storage. Forced Solar Charge prioritizes clean energy capture regardless of grid export rules. Need power fast? Forced Active Charge draws from the grid at maximum velocity to prepare for upcoming outages or high-use events.

Elite also features an Adjustable Shoulder Window—allowing you to customize peak/off-peak timing because convenience matters more than just the cheapest rate.

Smart Analytics

Precision Charge Timing

Know exactly when you'll be ready. Elite provides real-time estimates for Time to Full and Time to Empty based on your current home load. Review your performance with a Daily Energy Summary that simplifies complex data into actionable insights.

Always-On Command Center

Designed for dedicated monitoring stations. Enable Screen Always On to transform any device into a permanent wall-mounted energy terminal. With Persistent Login and Adjustable Text Sizes, your data is always visible and readable from across the room.

Viewing Mode: Simply Double Tap the dashboard to enter a clean, focused viewing mode—perfect for hands-free monitoring.
Professional Management

Multi-Site Monitoring

Manage your entire energy footprint by toggling between properties linked to your Tesla account. While each instance of Elite focuses on a single site for maximum precision, the app is designed for high-performance multi-window use.

Windows Multi-Window Support: To monitor and command multiple sites simultaneously, hold SHIFT + Left Click on the app icon or a property link to launch a new independent window for each location.
Data Intelligence

Deep Analytics & CSV Export

Your energy data belongs to you. Elite captures high-resolution historical graphs of your solar production, grid usage, and battery cycles. Need to run your own numbers? Use the one-click CSV Export to download your raw energy logs. Perfect for spreadsheet wizards who want to track long-term ROI or audit utility bill accuracy.

Live Graphing: Includes daily and monthly graphs that automatically update every 5 minutes while viewing to ensure your historical data is always current.
